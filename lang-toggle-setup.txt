  ---
  What the toggle does in plain English:

  Right now every page has text like "Add to cart", "Your cart is empty", "Checkout" etc. baked directly into the PHP files. A
  Khmer-speaking buyer sees English everywhere.

  The goal is: when a buyer clicks the 🇰🇭 flag in the header, those words switch to Khmer. Click 🇺🇸 and they switch back to
  English. That's it.

  ---
  What we've built so far (nothing visible has changed yet):

  What "wiring it in" looks like (the incremental part):

  Right now a page might say:

  After wiring, it says:
  <h2><?= $t['home_featured'] ?></h2>
